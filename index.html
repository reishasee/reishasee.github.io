About Me

I’m a structural engineer with nearly 5 years of professional experience in designing and analyzing low- to mid-rise buildings, alongside 2 years of academic research in South Korea. My expertise spans a wide range of tools and platforms, including ETABS, MIDAS Gen, SAFE, Revit, Navisworks, AutoCAD, and ABAQUS for finite element analysis—particularly in simulating corrosion in tensile specimens. I’m well-versed in international structural codes such as ACI 318-19M, ASCE 7-16, SBC 301, IBC 2018, and NSCP 2015, ensuring that all my designs meet rigorous safety and performance standards. With a strong analytical mindset and meticulous attention to detail, I take pride in delivering innovative, cost-effective, and code-compliant solutions. I’m known for being a collaborative and adaptable professional, staying ahead of evolving technologies and industry practices. My commitment to quality and client satisfaction consistently drives me to deliver high-caliber work that stands the test of time.
